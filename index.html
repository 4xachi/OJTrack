Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Does OJTrack require an active internet connection?
No! OJTrack is built 100% offline-first using local device storage. You can log shifts, view timelines, and calculate progress without internet access.
How does the Philippine Holiday double-hours calculation work?
When you log a shift under the "Holiday" status, OJTrack automatically applies a 2.0x multiplier to your worked hours (e.g., 8 hours worked on a holiday credits 16 completed hours toward your target).
Is my OJT data secure and private?
Yes! All your log entries, notes, and profile data remain strictly on your local device database. No personal logs are uploaded to any external server.
